# Respective Tomorrow (RT)
**Category**: Multiplayer Boss-Rush Quest (Episode 2)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Slay all four Episode 2 bosses in sequence.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **VR Temple**: Clear path of Diminians, warp, and slay **Barba Ray**.
2. **VR Spaceship**: Navigate Spaceship corridors, warp, and slay **Gol Dragon**.
3. **CCA**: Clear Gibbons and Mountain Olt, warp, and slay **Gal Gryphon**.
4. **Seabed**: Navigate Seabed Upper/Lower, warp, and slay **Olga Flow**.
5. **Evaluation**: Report to the Lab for final evaluation.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Barba Ray, Gol Dragon, Gal Gryphon, Olga Flow, and path mobs.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
RT is highly challenging due to Olga Flow and Gal Gryphon. Bring maximum Scape Dolls and equip high dark-resistance gear.
