# Government Task 8-2
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 8 - Seabed)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach the bottom-most levels of the Seabed labs.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Lower**: Warp to Seabed Lower.
2. **Corridor Sweeps**: Navigate corridors filled with Delbiters, Morfoses, and Delsabers.
3. **Override Seals**: Override three containment seals to open the elevator, then report.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dolphons, Delbiters, Morfoses, Delsabers, Epsilon.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
This map has many traps. Carry Trap Visions or keep moving to avoid high-damage explosive mines.
