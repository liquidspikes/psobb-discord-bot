# Government Task 7-1
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 7 - Central Control Area)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Locate the dome entry point in the Central Control Area.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp Down**: Warp to the CCA hub.
2. **Jungle Slayers**: Fight through Merillias and Ul Gibbons.
3. **Inspect Seal**: Locate the central security dome, check data, and return to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Merillias, Ul Gibbons, Mountain Gibbons, Mountain Olt.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Gibbons throw rocks and inflict elemental status effects. Focus them first using ranged weapons.
