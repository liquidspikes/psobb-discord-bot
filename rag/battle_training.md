# Battle Training
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Forest)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Learn combat basics and rescue the rookie Hunter, Ash.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Briefing**: Talk to the client, then warp to Forest 1 with Rupert.
2. **Tutorials**: Follow Rupert's prompts to practice normal attacks, hard attacks, and combos.
3. **Find Ash**: Slay the Booma waves in the northern corridor, locate Ash cornered by Boomas, and clear them.
4. **Escort**: Guide Ash safely to the exit teleporter.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Boomas, Savage Wolves, Rag Rappies.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Ash can heal you occasionally, but keep an eye on his HP so he doesn't get KO'd by Boomas.
