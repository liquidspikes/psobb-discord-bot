# Government Task 7-2
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 7 - Central Control Area)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Find the Jungle Dome Switch in the Jungle Sector.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Jungle Entry**: Enter the Jungle Sector path.
2. **Switch Search**: Navigate the outdoor maze.
3. **Activate**: Locate and activate the Jungle Dome Switch in the north clearing, then warp back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Merillias, Gee, Ul Gibbons, Mericar.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Merillias can jump and trap you. Freeze them with ice magic (Rabarta) or hit them from range.
