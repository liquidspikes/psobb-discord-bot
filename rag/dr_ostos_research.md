# Dr. Osto's Research
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Caves)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Retrieve data from Osto's hidden lab accompanied by Sue.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Lab Entry**: Warp to Caves 2 with Sue.
2. **Clearing Rooms**: Clear out heavy waves of Poison Lilies and Nano Dragons.
3. **Terminal Hack**: Access Osto's central database. Slay the final wave of Grass Assassins and report back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Sharks, Poison Lilies, Nano Dragons, Grass Assassins.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Sue is a HUnewearl who deals decent damage. Let her tank the sharks while you pick off the Poison Lilies from a distance.
