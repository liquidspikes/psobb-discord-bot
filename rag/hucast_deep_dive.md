# HUcast (Android Male Hunter) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **HUcast** is the "Absolute Powerhouse" of PSOBB, boasting the highest natural **ATP (Attack Power)** and **HP** in the game. As an Android, he cannot use Techniques (no Resta, Shifta, Deband, etc.), but he compensates with powerful **Traps**, built-in **Trap Vision**, and **natural HP regeneration** while standing still. He also receives a **30% bonus** to the success rates of Freeze, Paralysis, and Confuse weapon specials.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this efficient blueprint which assumes the use of **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1639 (Highest in game)
- **Max ATA:** 191
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 601
- **Max EVP:** 613

### Material Distribution (250 Cap)
- **Power Materials:** 105
- **Luck Materials:** 45
- **Defense/Mind/Evade:** 0 (Androids cannot use Mind materials anyway).
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Always max this separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Build:** 5 DEF / 145 POW / 50 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Recommended Evolution:** **Sato**, **Nidra**, or **Tellusis** (Tellusis has hidden synergy with Excalibur).
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**. This is the only way for a HUcast to get high-level Shifta/Deband buffs (up to Lv. 81 with 4-way PB) without other players.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **The Boss Killer:** *Dark Flow* (Special attack waves deal massive damage when your HP is below 12.5%).
- **Primary Melee:** *Excalibur* (Incredible one-handed damage).
- **High Damage Nuke:** *Vjaya* (Consumes Meseta for extreme damage special).
- **Multi-Target Ranged:** *Diska of Braveman* (Requires high Hit% and high ATP).
- **Single-Target Ranged:** *L&K38 Combat* (High DPS ranged option).
- **S-Rank Utility:** *S-Rank Twin* or *S-Rank Scythe* with **Zalure** (to lower enemy defense) or **Arrest**.

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Thirteen* (ATP boost) or *D-Parts ver1.01* (ATP/ATA boost for Androids).
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for stats and ATA).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Attack speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **Centurion/Ability:** Massive boost to all stats to help reach max caps.
3. **Adept:** Used for resistance boosts and topping off ATA/stats.
4. **V502:** Increases the success rate of status specials (Freeze, Arrest, Hell). Note: HUcast already gets a 30% bonus to Freeze/Arrest.

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Skyly (Primary):** The definitive "Hunter ID." Best for finding *Sealed J-Sword*, *Chain Sawd*, and other melee rares.
- **Redria:** High utility. Finds *God/Battle*, *Red Ring*, and *high-slot armors*.
- **Purplenum:** Good for finding *Mechguns* and *Psycho Wand* (for trading).

## 5. Advanced Combat & Trap Strategies
- **Freeze Traps (MVP):** Use these to "freeze-lock" dangerous enemies like Dorphons, Delbiters, or large groups of mobs. This is your primary crowd control.
- **Confuse Traps:** Great for chaos in large rooms; enemies will target each other instead of you.
- **Trap-Shooting:** Place a trap and shoot it with a handgun/mechgun to detonate it instantly. Useful for clearing low-HP mobs or revealing hidden enemies.
- **HP Management:** Since you cannot use Resta, carry **Trimate** and **Star Atomizer** at all times. Use your natural HP regen during lulls in combat.
- **The Mylla & Youlla Rule:** In solo play, wait for your PB gauge to reach 100% and use M&Y at the start of a difficult room for a massive power spike.
- **Role Mastery:** Your job is to be the **front-line tank and DPS**. Use your traps to make the battlefield safe for your teammates while you delete high-priority targets.
