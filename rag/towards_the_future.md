# Towards the Future (TTF)
**Category**: Multiplayer Boss-Rush Quest (Episode 1)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Slay all four Episode 1 bosses in a fast speedrun.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Forest**: Clear Boomas and Hildebears, step into the portal, and slay the **Dragon**.
2. **Caves**: Navigate the brief magma channel, step into the portal, and slay **De Rol Le**.
3. **Mines**: Clear Gillchics and Garanz, step into the portal, and slay **Vol Opt**.
4. **Ruins**: Navigate the final hallway of Dimenians, warp, and slay **Dark Falz**.
5. **Evaluation**: Settle rewards based on speed and survival rank.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dragon, De Rol Le, Vol Opt, Dark Falz, and quick path mobs.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
TTF is the best leveling quest in Episode 1. Carry Trap Visions for the Mines sector. Coordinate with a Force for Shifta/Deband.
