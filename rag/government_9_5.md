# Government Task 9-5
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Crater)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Descend into Crater Interior and locate the Desert entrance.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Interior Warp**: Warp to Crater Interior.
2. **Heavy Sweeps**: Slay Astarks and Lizards in the deep caverns.
3. **Gate Unlock**: Activate the ancient gate switches, defeat the guardians, and return.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x02C1`, unlocking Subterranean Desert 1!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Astarks, Sand Lizards, Bootas, Darcly.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
This sector is packed with high-HP enemies. Coordinate with a Force for buffs (Shifta/Deband) to clear rooms efficiently.
