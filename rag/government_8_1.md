# Government Task 8-1
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 8 - Seabed)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Investigate the upper flooded sectors of the Seabed.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Seabed**: Warp to Seabed Upper.
2. **Slay Hounds**: Defeat Delbiters and Morfoses.
3. **Core Power**: Clear the filtration tank room, activate the main power matrix, and report back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dolphons, Delbiters, Morfoses, Recobox.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Delbiters have a high-speed charge and a powerful laser beam. Freeze them with Rabarta/Barta immediately to stop their charges.
