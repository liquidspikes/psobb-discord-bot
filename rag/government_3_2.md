# Government Task 3-2
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 3 - Mines)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Explore Mines 2 and disable the secondary defensive network node.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Mines 2 Entry**: Warp to Mines 2.
2. **Minefields**: Watch the floors carefully; there are invisible explosive landmines. Carry Trap Visions to spot them.
3. **Canadine Circle**: Slay the Canadines surrounding the Canane leader in the grid chamber.
4. **Node Override**: Defeat the Garanz guarding the final terminal room, override the defensive node, and report back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gillchics, Canadines, Canane, Garanz, Sinow Beats.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Garanz fire homing missiles. When they fire, run around them in tight circles so the missiles hit the ground, and stay away when they die, as they detonate.
