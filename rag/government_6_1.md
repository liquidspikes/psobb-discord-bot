# Government Task 6-1
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 6 - VR Spaceship)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach VR Spaceship Alpha and override local security.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Alpha Entry**: Warp to VR Spaceship Alpha.
2. **Grid Traps**: Navigate electric grids and wall laser traps.
3. **Warp Out**: Slay Wulfs and Gilchichs blocking the exit terminal, then report.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Wulfs, Gilchichs, Dubchichs, Canadines.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Shoot wall-mounted laser triggers before walking through doors to avoid taking high damage.
