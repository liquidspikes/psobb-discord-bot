# Government Task 7-4
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 7 - Central Control Area)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Locate the Seaside Dome Switch in the Seaside Sector.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Seaside Path**: Enter the Seaside Sector.
2. **Beach Run**: Navigate beaches and stone bridges.
3. **Switch Hack**: Slay Gi Gues and Merikle, activate the switch, and return.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Ul Gibbons, Gee, Gi Gues, Merikle.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Gi Gues project temporary energy barriers. Wait for the barrier to drop or strike them with lightning magic (Zonde).
