# Government Task 6-5
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 6 - VR Spaceship)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Defeat the Gol Dragon and clear the simulation.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Boss**: Warp to the Spaceship Boss Portal.
2. **Defeat Gol Dragon**: Slay the dragon and its duplicates.
3. **Victory**: Report to the Lab.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x0225`, unlocking the Central Control Area (CCA) in Free Play!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gol Dragon Boss.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Stand next to its feet to attack, and retreat when it stomps. In its illusion phase, only attack the real dragon (the one casting normal attacks). Run in circles when it flies.
