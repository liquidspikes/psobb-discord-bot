# HUcaseal (Android Female Hunter) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **HUcaseal** is the "Precision Specialist" of the Hunter class. While she has lower ATP than the HUcast, she possesses the **highest natural ATA (Accuracy) and EVP (Evade)** of all Hunters. Her unique weapon animations for Daggers, Double Sabers, Fists, and Claws are significantly faster than other classes. As an Android, she has built-in **Trap Vision**, **natural HP regeneration**, and a **30% bonus** to the success rates of Freeze, Paralysis, and Confuse weapon specials.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this plan which assumes the use of **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1301
- **Max ATA:** 218 (Highest for Hunters)
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 525
- **Max EVP:** 877 (Highest for Hunters)

### Material Distribution (150 Cap - Android Limit)
- **Power Materials:** 62
- **Defense Materials:** 53
- **Evade Materials:** 7
- **Luck Materials:** 28
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Always max this separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Build:** 5 DEF / 131 POW / 64 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**. This is her primary source of Shifta/Deband buffs for solo play.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **The "Stun-Lock":** *Daylight Scar* or *Jizai* (Takes advantage of her unique, ultra-fast Dagger/Twin animations).
- **Primary Melee:** *Excalibur* (3-target damage).
- **High Damage Nuke:** *Vjaya* (Consumes Meseta for extreme damage special).
- **Ranged DPS:** *M&A60 Vise* (Mechguns; her high ATA makes these devastating).
- **Precision Specials:** *Raygun* or *Slicer of Fanatic* with **Hell**, **Arrest**, or **Demon's** (Takes advantage of her near-Ranger ATA).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Thirteen* (ATA boost) or *D-Parts ver1.01* (Android exclusive; adds +35 ATP).
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for all stats).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **V502:** Increases the success rate of Hell, Arrest, and Freeze specials. Critical for HUcaseal's status-inflicting role.
3. **Smartlink:** Mandatory for Hunters using ranged weapons in Ultimate difficulty.
4. **Centurion/Ability:** Rounding out any remaining stats.

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Purplenum (Primary):** Drops *M&A60 Vise* (essential ranged weapon) and high-end Mechguns.
- **Skyly:** The definitive Hunter ID. Best for finding *Excalibur* and *Sealed J-Sword*.
- **Viridia:** Good for *Red Ring* and *Parasitic Gene "Flow"* hunts.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Trap Strategies
- **Precision Status Inflicting:** Because your ATA is near-Ranger levels, you can use **Hell** and **Arrest** weapons much more reliably than any other Hunter. You are the premier "Status Hunter."
- **Trap Mastery:** Use **Freeze Traps** to lock down mobs, then shred them with your fast Dagger animations. Use **Confuse Traps** in crowded rooms to turn enemies against each other.
- **Trap-Shooting:** Drop and shoot traps instantly to clear paths and neutralize threats before they reach your team.
- **The Animation Advantage:** Abuse your superior speed with *Daggers* and *Double Sabers* to "stun-lock" single targets, preventing them from attacking at all.
- **Team Role:** You are the **Sturdy Controller and Precision Melee Dealer**. You provide unmatched crowd control via traps and status effects while dealing consistent, fast melee damage.
