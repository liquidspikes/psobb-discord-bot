# PSOBB Advanced Hints & Strategies (Source: PSO-World / Community)

## 1. Advanced Combat Mechanics
- **Optimal Combos:** The standard efficiency combo is **Normal-Hard-Hard (N-H-H)**. In Ultimate difficulty, timing is critical to stun-lock enemies.
- **Barehand Casting:** Male characters (HUmar, RAmar, FOmar, FOnewm) cast support techniques significantly faster when no weapon is equipped. Hotkey your weapon to unequip/equip for rapid buffing.
- **SN Glitch (Special-Normal):** Using a Normal attack before a Special attack (on the same or a different weapon) can carry over accuracy bonuses, making low-accuracy specials like *Arrest* or *Hell* more reliable.
- **Damage Cancel (DMC):** In multiplayer, overlapping attacks on the same frame can "cancel" damage. Coordinate with teammates to space out high-damage hits.

## 2. Endgame Gearing & Items
- **The "Hit" Stat:** In Ultimate difficulty, **Accuracy (ATA)** is the primary bottleneck. A common weapon with 50% Hit is often superior to a rare weapon with 0% Hit. Always prioritize Hit % on endgame gear.
- **Unit Priorities:** 
  - **God/Battle** (or Heavenly/Battle): Increases attack speed. Mandatory for melee classes.
  - **God/Arm:** Increases ATA. Essential for reaching Max Stats.
  - **God/Ability:** Increases all stats. Great for overall balancing.
- **Section ID Optimization:**
  - **Skyly:** Best for Hunters (Sealed J-Sword).
  - **Purplenum:** Best for Rangers and Mechguns (Yas 9000M).
  - **Redria:** Best for Armor/Shields and high-end units.

## 3. Challenge Mode (C-Mode)
- **Role Mastery:** Success depends on map knowledge and teamwork. 
- **Item Sharing:** Drops are shared based on class needs (e.g., Rifles to Rangers, Sabers to Hunters). Hoarding items leads to team failure.
- **Trap Management:** Androids must take the lead to identify and destroy traps. Use **Freeze Traps** to neutralize dangerous mobs like Garanz or Sinows.
- **Speed is Key:** Avoid unnecessary combat. If a room doesn't require a "Clear" to proceed, skip it.

## 4. Rare Enemy Hunting
- **Rare Spawn Odds:** Rare variants (Mil Lily, Red Slime, etc.) have a ~1/500 chance to replace common spawns.
- **Slime Splitting:** You can split a Pouilly Slime into 4 by hitting it with a weak attack or a low-level Rabarta. Each split has an independent chance to be a **Red Slime**.
- **The "Pipe Trick" Warning:** The classic "Telepipe Trick" (piping back to the ship to reset spawns) **does not work** on most PSOBB servers. Spawns are fixed. You must restart the quest/room.
- **Best Quests for Hunting:**
  - **Rappies:** *Fake in Yellow* (Forest).
  - **Lilies:** *Addicting Food* (Caves).
  - **Hildes:** *Forest of Sorrow* (Forest).

## 5. Technical Tips
- **Mag Triggers:** Prioritize Mags like **Sato** or **Nidra** for their 50% chance of Invincibility triggers at 100% PB or 10% HP.
- **Technique Levels:** Level 30 is the max for Forces. Level 20 for HUnewearl/RAmarl. Level 15 for HUmar/RAmar.
