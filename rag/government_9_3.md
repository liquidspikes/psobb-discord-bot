# Government Task 9-3
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Crater)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach Crater South and investigate the meteorite crash site.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to South**: Warp down to Crater South.
2. **Collect Samples**: Search the impact craters for mineral deposits.
3. **Exit**: Defeat the Sand Lizards and report back.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x02BF`, unlocking Crater North!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Bootas, Astarks, Sand Lizards, Zu.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Sand Lizards block front-facing attacks. Use magic (which bypasses blocking) or maneuver behind them.
