# FOmar (Human Male Force) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **FOmar** is the "Battle Mage" of PSOBB. He possesses the highest **ATP (Attack Power)** among Forces and unique animations that make him viable for melee combat. He excels in solo play and offensive support, featuring powerful boosts to Gi-series techniques and a doubled range for Shifta and Deband.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
FOmar requires a careful balance to capitalize on his hybrid nature. This plan assumes **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1002 (Highest for Forces)
- **Max ATA:** 163
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 470
- **Max EVP:** 651
- **Max MST:** 1340

### Material Distribution (250 Cap)
- **Power Materials:** 117
- **Defense Materials:** 65
- **Mind Materials:** 16
- **Evade Materials:** 14
- **Luck Materials:** 38
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Hybrid Build:** 5 DEF / 0 POW / 46 DEX / 149 MIND (Relies on materials for ATP).
- **Melee Build:** 5 DEF / 120 POW / 50 DEX / 25 MIND (Focuses on raw melee damage).
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **Primary Casting:** *Psycho Wand* (Massive Ra-tech boost; consumes HP).
- **Gi-tech Specialist:** *Magical Piece* (+30% damage to Gifoie, Gizonde, Gibarta).
- **Melee DPS:** *Excalibur* (Excellent ATP for a Force).
- **Ranged Utility:** *Slicer of Fanatic* (Demon's special) or *Bringer's Rifle*.
- **Tech Utility:** *Dark Bridge* (Grants/Megid boost) or *Summit Moon*.

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Thirteen* (EVP boost) or *Ignition Cloak* (Fire boost).
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS) or *Gifoie Merge* (+30% Gifoie damage).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Mandatory for melee/casting hybrid.
2. **V801:** Essential for casting speed (replaces Heavenly/Mind/Battle for casting).
3. **Smartlink:** Removes the ATA penalty for ranged weapons (essential for Demon's specials).
4. **V502:** Increases success rate of status specials (Hell, Demon's).

## 4. Innate Class Boosts
- **Gi-series Techniques:** +30% Damage (Gifoie, Gizonde, Gibarta).
- **Grants:** +30% Damage.
- **Support Range:** 2x Range for **Shifta** and **Deband**.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Solo Strategies
- **Barehand Casting:** Unequip your weapon to cast Ra-series techniques faster. This is a core mechanic for high-level FOmar play.
- **The "Red Mage" Strategy:** Use **Gifoie** to stun-lock groups, then close in for melee with *Excalibur*.
- **Status Weaving:** FOmar has high enough ATA to land **Demon's** specials with a *Smartlink*. Use this to shred Boss HP before finishing with techs or melee.
- **Solo Survivability:** You are the most fragile human class. Keep **Jellen/Zalure** active at all times to compensate for low DFP.
- **Team Role:** Offensive Support. You provide the team with massive S/D coverage while contributing significant AoE damage and picking off high-HP targets with Demon's.
