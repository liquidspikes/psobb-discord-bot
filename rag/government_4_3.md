# Government Task 4-3
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 4 - Ruins)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Reach the bottom-most altar in Ruins 3 and deactivate the master seal.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Ruins 3 Entry**: Warp to Ruins 3.
2. **Centaur Ambush**: Defeat the Chaos Bringers charging through the initial corridors.
3. **Activate Master Console**: Reach the three-pronged alter and clear out the Delsaber and Chaos Sorcerer waves.
4. **Report**: Warp back and report to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dimenians, Chaos Sorcerers, Chaos Bringers, Dark Belras, Bulclaws.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Chaos Bringers charge and steal TP. Stand behind obstacles or use ice magic (Rabarta) to freeze them before they charge.
