# Government Task 3-3
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 3 - Mines)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Purge the Vol Opt core mainframe and secure entry to the Ruins.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Mainframe**: Warp to Mines 2 and step into the giant Boss Portal.
2. **Defeat Vol Opt**: Slay Vol Opt's Phase 1 computer monitor and Phase 2 giant robot.
3. **Victory**: Report to the Principal.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x0207`, unlocking the Ruins warp in Free Play and opening Tier 4 quests!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Vol Opt Boss.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
**Vol Opt Strategy**:
* **Phase 1**: Only attack the wall monitor that displays Vol Opt's red face. Avoid the crushing pillars and lasers.
* **Phase 2**: If locked in a cage of pillars, quickly break one pillar to escape the homing missile. Use Zonde (lightning) magic or heavy physical strikes to deal maximum damage.
