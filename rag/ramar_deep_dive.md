# RAmar (Human Male Ranger) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **RAmar** is the "Ultimate Sniper" of PSOBB, boasting the highest natural ATA (Accuracy) in the game. This makes him the most reliable user of "Special" attacks like Hell, Demon's, and Arrest. While he lacks the raw damage of android Rangers, his accuracy and support techniques make him a top-tier endgame character.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this plan which assumes the use of **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1260
- **Max ATA:** 249
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 478
- **Max EVP:** 763
- **Max MST:** 680

### Material Distribution (250 Cap)
- **Power Materials:** 53
- **Defense Materials:** 56
- **Mind Materials:** 63
- **Evade Materials:** 21
- **Luck Materials:** 28
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Standard Build:** 5 DEF / 157 POW / 38 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Special Evolution:** **Striker Unit** (Required to unlock the long-range "Divine Punishment" special on the *Heaven Striker* weapon).
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)** for Level 21+ Shifta/Deband during solo play.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **Crowd Control:** *Spread Needle* (Essential for paralyzing and slowing groups).
- **The "Nuke":** *Heaven Striker* (Use with *Striker Unit* Mag for extreme range special).
- **Single Target DPS:** *Charge Vulcans* (50%+ Hit) or *Guld Milla*.
- **Freezing:** *Frozen Shooter* (100% Freeze rate) or *Snow Queen*.
- **Special Inflicting:** *Bringer's Rifle* (Demon's special), *Raygun/Laser* with Hell or Arrest (50%+ Hit).
- **Melee Utility:** *Excalibur* (For high ATP melee when range isn't needed).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *DF Field*, *Brightness Circle*, or *Aura Field*.
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for ATA and stats), *Stand Still Shield* (Pure DFP), or *Ranger Wall*.

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **V502:** Increases the success rate of Hell, Arrest, and Freeze specials. Critical for RAmar's high-ATA gameplay.
3. **Adept:** Used for resistance boosts and topping off ATA/stats.
4. **Centurion/Ability:** Rounding out any remaining stats to reach max.

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Purplenum (Primary):** The "Ranger ID." Hunts *Yasminkov 9000M*, *Psycho Wand*, and general Mechguns.
- **Redria:** High utility. Finds *Spread Needle*, *Red Ring*, and high-end Armor/Units.
- **Whitill:** Excellent all-rounder. Hunts *Heaven Striker* and *Guld Milla* pieces.

## 5. Advanced Combat Strategies
- **Special Inflicting:** Use your massive ATA to land **Hell** (instant death) on non-robot enemies and **Demon's** (reduces HP to 25%) on high-HP targets and bosses.
- **The Frozen Shooter Rule:** Always keep a *Frozen Shooter* on your palette. Freeze dangerous targets like Delbiters or Mini-bosses before they reach the team.
- **Self-Buffing:** RAmar is limited to Level 15 Shifta/Deband. Use a Mag with **Mylla & Youlla** to trigger Level 21+ buffs, or carry *S-Red's Blade* to cast Level 3 S/D in a pinch.
- **Trap Detection:** While not an Android, RAmar's high ATA allows him to clear traps effectively with a handgun or *Spread Needle* once they are revealed.
- **Team Role:** In a party, your job is **Status Inflicting and Support**. Keep enemies paralyzed/frozen while dealing consistent chip damage.
