# Government Task 5-1
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 5 - VR Temple)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Complete initial recon of VR Temple Alpha.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Lab Start**: Speak to Nol, then warp down to VR Temple Alpha.
2. **Switch Puzzle**: Step on the dual floor switches with your party to open the eastern gates.
3. **Clear Rappies**: Slay the Rag Rappy swarms blocking the exit teleporter.
4. **Report**: Return to the Lab.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Diminians, Rag Rappies, El Rappies, Poison Lilies.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Use fire magic (Foie/Rafoie) to incinerate Rappies and Diminian swarms quickly.
