# Government Task 5-2
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 5 - VR Temple)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Locate and sync the system terminal in VR Temple Beta.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Beta Entry**: Warp to VR Temple Beta.
2. **Lily Hallways**: Slay the Poison Lilies in the tight corridors.
3. **Terminal Hack**: Find three lock switches to unlock the central terminal.
4. **Report**: Sync terminal and return.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Diminians, Grass Assassins, Poison Lilies, Hildebears.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Slay Poison Lilies from a distance using a Rifle or Handgun to avoid paralysis.
