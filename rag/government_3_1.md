# Government Task 3-1
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 3 - Mines)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach Mines 1 and deactivate the emergency security lockdown.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Mines 1 Entry**: Warp down to Mines 1.
2. **Dubchic Rooms**: You will face Dubchics. Navigate to the eastern corridor.
3. **Security Terminal**: Step on the floor switch hidden behind the crates to open the secure gate.
4. **Unlock Mainframe**: Slay the Sinow Beats in the central server room, interact with the security terminal, and return to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gillchics, Dubchics, Sinow Beats, Canadines, Canane.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Dubchics cannot be killed by normal attacks; they will revive indefinitely. Search the room for the floating red Dubchic Switch (sensor orb) and destroy it to deactivate all Dubchics instantly.
