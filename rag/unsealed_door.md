# Unsealed Door
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Caves)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Rescue survivors in the Caves and escort Mome.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Magma Tunnels**: Warp down to Caves 1 and 2.
2. **Clear Rooms**: Clear corrupt Cave monsters to open doors.
3. **Escort**: Locate Mome in Caves 2 and escort him safely to the Caves 3 warp.
4. **Report**: Warp back and report.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Sharks, Poison Lilies, Nano Dragons, Grass Assassins.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Mome has very low HP. Keep him behind you and freeze sharks with Barta/Rabarta so they don't target him.
