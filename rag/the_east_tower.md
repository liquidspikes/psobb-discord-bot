# The East Tower
**Category**: Multiplayer High-Difficulty Quest (Episode 2 Tower)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Ascend the eastern Control Tower and retrieve weather data.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Tower Entry**: Warp directly to the Control Tower sector.
2. **Ascend Floors**: Ascend 5 floors of extremely dense, high-difficulty combat.
3. **Slay Tower Mobs**: Defeat Ill Gills, Delbiters, and Epsilons.
4. **Sync**: Access the terminal on the roof and return.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Ill Gills, Delbiters, Epsilons, Mericars.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Ill Gills can charge and cast Megid; freeze them immediately with Rabarta or Freeze Traps. Epsilons reflect damage; only strike them when their shield matches the opposite element of your attack.
