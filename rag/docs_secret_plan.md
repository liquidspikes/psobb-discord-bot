# Doc's Secret Plan
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Mines)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Complete a special training simulation and unlock Enemy Parts.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Mines Warp**: Warp to Mines 2.
2. **Timed Fights**: Clear three waves of Canadines and Sinow Beats within the time limit.
3. **Unlock**: Return to Pioneer 2. **Enemy Part Weapon Conversion is now permanently unlocked!**

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Canadines, Gillchics, Sinow Beats.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
To convert an Enemy Part (e.g. Delsaber's Arm) after clearing this, load 'Dr. Osto's Research' or 'Unsealed Door' in any difficulty, and speak to Dr. Montague standing in Pioneer 2.
