## Rare Enemy & Boss Notes

### Rare Spawns
*   **Al Rappy:** Blue variant of Rag Rappy (Forest).
*   **Love Rappy:** Pink variant of Rag Rappy (VR Temple/Episode 2).
*   **Hildeblue:** Blue variant of Hildebear (Forest/VR Temple).
*   **Nar Lily:** Red variant of Poison Lily (Caves/VR Temple).
*   **Mil Lily:** Rainbow variant of Poison Lily (Ruins).
*   **Pouilly Slime:** Red variant of Pofuilly Slime (Caves).
*   **Pazuzu:** Green/Winged variant of Zu (Crater).
*   **Dorphon Eclair:** Spiked/Dark variant of Dorphon (Crater).
*   **Kondrieu:** A rare white variant of the Saint-Million/Shambertin boss in the Subterranean Desert.

### Area Specifics
*   **Ep. 2 Towers:** These are primarily accessed via specific quests like "The East Tower" and "The West Tower".
*   **Ep. 4 Bosses:** The boss spawn at the end of Episode IV depends on the route taken and RNG (Kondrieu is rare).
