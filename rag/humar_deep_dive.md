# HUmar (Human Male Hunter) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **HUmar** is the quintessential "balanced" Hunter. While he lacks the raw power and traps of the HUcast, he compensates with Level 15 techniques and high accuracy potential. In the endgame, he is often played as a high-sustained DPS "Glass Cannon."

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this standard blueprint assuming the use of a **V101** unit:
- **Max ATP:** 1397
- **Max ATA:** 200
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 579
- **Max EVP:** 756
- **Max MST:** 732

### Material Distribution (250 Cap)
- **Power Materials:** 82
- **Luck Materials:** 45
- **Defense/Mind/Evade:** 0 (Use the remaining 123 slots for HP Materials or extra Power if swapping units).
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap of 125).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Build:** 5 DEF / 147 POW / 48 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Recommended Evolution:** **Diwari** or **Varaha**.
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)** for essential Shifta/Deband buffs.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **Crowd Control:** *Tsumikiri J-Sword* (Unsealed) or *Dark Flow*.
- **Boss Killer:** *Dark Flow* (Special attack deals massive damage at low HP).
- **Single Target:** *Excalibur* or *Vjaya* (Requires Meseta for Special).
- **Ranged Utility:** *Charge Vulcans* (50%+ Hit) or *Red Handgun*.
- **Self-Buffing:** *S-Red's Blade* (Special casts Level 3 Shifta/Deband).
- **Tech Utility:** *Glide Divine* (Increases Jellen/Zalure range).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *DF Field* or *Aura Field*. *Thirteen* is a viable alternative for stat boosting.
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for stats/ATA), *Kasami Bracer* (ATP boost), or *Stand Still Shield* (Pure DFP).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **Smartlink:** Mandatory for Hunters using ranged weapons in Ultimate difficulty (negates accuracy penalty).
3. **V502:** Increases the success rate of status specials (Hell, Freeze, Arrest).
4. **Adept** or **Centurion/Ability:** Used to round out any remaining stats to reach max.

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Skyly (Primary):** The "Hunter ID." Finds *Sealed J-Sword*, *Chain Sawd*, and *Daylight Scar*.
- **Redria:** High utility. Finds *God/Battle*, *Red Ring*, and high-slot armors.
- **Viridia:** Good for *Red Ring* and Partisan drops.

## 5. Advanced Combat Strategies
- **Technique Support:** Keep **Zalure** (Level 15) on enemies at all times to maximize your ATP. Use **Jellen** (Level 15) to mitigate your lower DFP.
- **Self-Sufficiency:** Since HUmar cannot cast Shifta/Deband, you **must** rely on:
  - **S-Red's Blade** special for Level 3 S/D.
  - **Mylla & Youlla** PB for Level 21+ S/D.
- **Combo Management:** Use **Normal-Hard-Hard (N-H-H)** for most mobs. If accuracy is an issue, start with **Normal-Normal-Hard (N-N-H)**.
- **Android Comparison:** Unlike HUcast, you have no traps. You must use weapons with **Arrest** or **Blizzard** specials to control large groups.
