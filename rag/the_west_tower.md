# The West Tower
**Category**: Multiplayer High-Difficulty Quest (Episode 2 Tower)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Ascend the western Control Tower and disable the security AI.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Tower Entry**: Enter the Control Tower.
2. **Ascend**: Navigate floors filled with Mericars, Mericlones, Delbiters, and Ill Gills.
3. **Shutdown**: Deactivate the security node on the roof floor.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Ill Gills, Delbiters, Epsilons, Mericlones, Mericlanes.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Mericlones spit high-damage sludge that deals instant death. Keep your distance and slay them from range using a Rifle.
