# PSOBB Mag Feeding & Evolution Reference (Source: ragol.co.uk)

## Core Mechanics
- **Feeding Timer:** Feed up to 3 items every 3 minutes and 30 seconds.
- **Max Level:** 200.
- **Stats:**
  - **DEF (Defense):** Increases DFP. (1 DEF = 1 DFP)
  - **POW (Power):** Increases ATP. (1 POW = 2 ATP)
  - **DEX (Dexterity):** Increases ATA. (1 DEX = 0.5 ATA)
  - **MIND (Mind):** Increases MST. (1 MIND = 2 MST)

## Feeding Strategy (Optimization)
- **Hunters/Rangers:** Aim for high POW and enough DEX to hit enemies in Ultimate (target ~50 DEX). Keep DEF at the base (5 or 10).
- **Forces:** Aim for high MIND and ~50 DEX.
- **Common Items:**
  - **Monomate/Dimate:** Increases POW, decreases MIND/DEX.
  - **Monofluid/Difluid:** Increases MIND, decreases POW/DEX.
  - **Antidote:** Increases DEX, decreases POW/MIND.
  - **Antiparalysis:** Increases DEX, decreases POW/MIND (alt ratio).
  - **Sol Atomizer:** Balanced growth across multiple stats.

## Evolution Milestones
Evolution depends on the **Class**, **Gender**, and **Section ID** of the character feeding it.

### Level 10 (1st Evolution)
- **Hunter:** Varuna (PB: Farlla)
- **Ranger:** Kalki (PB: Estlla)
- **Force:** Vritra (PB: Leilla)

### Level 35 (2nd Evolution)
- Depends on the highest stat:
  - **POW > DEX > MIND:** Rudra (H), Surya (R), Ashvin (F)
  - **DEX > POW > MIND:** Marutah (H), Tapas (R), Namuci (F)
  - **MIND > POW > DEX:** Vayu (H), Mitra (R), Sumba (F)

### Level 50 (3rd Evolution)
- This is where Section ID groupings matter. Group A (Viridia, Skyly, Purplenum, Redria, Yellowboze) vs. Group B (Greenill, Bluefull, Pinkal, Oran, Whitill).
- **Key Mag: Sato (Special)**
  - Requires: Female Force, Group B ID.
  - Stats: DEF + DEX = POW + MIND.
  - Triggers: Invincibility at 100% PB and 10% HP. Highly prized.

## Photon Blasts (PB)
- **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y):** The most important PB. Provides Shifta & Deband buffs.
- **Estlla:** High damage line attack.
- **Pilla:** Powerful AoE centered on the player.
- **Farlla:** Circular attack around the player.
- **Golla:** Single-target lightning strike.
- **Leilla:** Healing (Resta) effect.

## Pro Tips
- Use a **Level 10-50 Calculator** to ensure you get the right Photon Blasts.
- To change a Mag's 3rd evolution, give it to a character with a different Class/ID and feed it until it reaches a level divisible by 5 (e.g., Level 55, 60).
- Special "Cell Mags" (like **Chu Chu**, **RoboChao**, **Nidra**) require specific items and stat conditions.
