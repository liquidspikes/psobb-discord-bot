# Government Task 7-5
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 7 - Central Control Area)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Slay the Gal Gryphon inside the dome.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Dome**: Teleport to the CCA Dome and enter the Boss Portal.
2. **Slay Gryphon**: Defeat Gal Gryphon.
3. **Victory**: Report to the Lab.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x022F`, unlocking Seabed in Free Play!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gal Gryphon Boss.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
When it glides low, run to the far left or right to avoid it. When it lands, stay back to avoid circular shockwaves, then attack its head. Run between tornadoes.
