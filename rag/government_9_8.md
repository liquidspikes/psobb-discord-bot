# Government Task 9-8
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Desert)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach the final sand cavern and defeat Saint-Million.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Desert 3 Entry**: Warp to Subterranean Desert 3 and enter the sandstorm Boss Portal.
2. **Slay Saint-Million**: Defeat the giant worm boss (or Shambertin/Kondrieu).
3. **Victory**: Report back to complete the Episode 4 storyline!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Saint-Million Boss (or Shambertin / Kondrieu).

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Run far left/right to dodge its giant sand laser. Avoid its spin tail swipe. Attack its chest crystal core when it opens to deal triple damage.
