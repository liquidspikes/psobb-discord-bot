# FOnewm (Newman Male Force) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **FOnewm** is the "Black Mage" of PSOBB, the ultimate Area-of-Effect (AoE) specialist. He receives a massive damage boost for all multi-target techniques (Gi-series and Ra-series), making him the most efficient room-clearer among Forces. As a Newman, he also benefits from natural TP regeneration while standing still.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
FOnewm focuses heavily on MST and ATA to land powerful tech damage and occasional status specials.
- **Max ATP:** 814
- **Max ATA:** 180
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 463
- **Max EVP:** 679
- **Max MST:** 1500

### Material Distribution (150 Cap - Newman Limit)
- **Mind Materials:** 92
- **Power Materials:** 35
- **Luck Materials:** 15
- **Evade Materials:** 8
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap).
- **TP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Nuker Build:** 5 DEF / 88 POW / 61 DEX / 46 MIND
- **Alternative Build:** 5 DEF / 0 POW / 45 DEX / 150 MIND (Pure MST focus).
- **Recommended Evolution:** **Sato** or **Nidra**.
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **Primary Nuking:** *Psycho Wand* (Essential for Ra-techs; reduces TP cost via HP).
- **Gi-tech Specialist:** *Magical Piece* (+30% Gi-series damage).
- **Single Target Utility:** *Dark Bridge* (+30% Grants/Megid boost).
- **Ranged Utility:** *Slicer of Fanatic* or *Bringer's Rifle* (Demon's special).
- **Tech Utility:** *Summit Moon* (+30% Simple-series damage).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Thirteen* (EVP focus) or *Aura Field*.
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS all stats) or *Ra-tech Merges* (Rafoie/Rabarta/Razonde Merge for +30% damage).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **V801:** Essential for casting speed.
3. **V502:** For landing Demon's or Hell specials.
4. **Smartlink:** Mandatory for landing Demon's specials with Ranger weapons.

## 4. Innate Class Boosts
- **Gi-series Techniques:** +30% Damage.
- **Ra-series Techniques:** +30% Damage.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Solo Strategies
- **The "Black Mage" Strategy:** Position yourself in the center of a mob and spam **Ra-techs** to hit everything at once. Use the **Rafoie/Rabarta/Razonde Merges** for specific element boosts.
- **Barehand Casting:** Unequip your weapon to cast Ra-series techniques significantly faster. This is vital for maximizing AoE DPS.
- **TP Management:** As a Newman, stay still between spawns to regenerate TP. Use **TP materials** early to increase your pool.
- **Demon's Weaving:** With high ATA and a **Smartlink**, use *Slicer of Fanatic* to lower enemy HP before finishing with techs.
- **Team Role:** AoE Nuker and Crowd Controller. You are responsible for clearing out trash mobs and debuffing large groups with Jellen/Zalure.
