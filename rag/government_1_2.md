# Government Task 1-2
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 1 - Forest)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Explore Forest 2 and recover scientific telemetry data from the ancient Forest Monument.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Pioneer 2 Start**: Get briefed by the Principal and warp directly to Forest 2.
2. **Clearing the Passages**: Fight through the initial narrow canyon. Hildebears are stationed at critical checkpoints—they must be completely defeated to unlock the security doors.
3. **Junction Puzzle**: At the central fork, head east to find a target button on the wall. Hit it with a physical strike or ranged attack to lower the gate on the western path.
4. **The Monument Clearing**: Enter the massive open clearing in the north. Slay the Monests (beehives) quickly to stop them from infinitely spawning Mothmants.
5. **Data Retrieval**: Slay the two guarding Goboomas, approach the central glowing ancient Monument, and interact with the terminal to record the monument's data.
6. **Debriefing**: Teleport back to Pioneer 2 and speak to the Principal to complete the quest.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Boomas, Goboomas, Hildebears, Monests, Mothmants, Savage Wolves.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Prioritize destroying the Monests (hive structures) from long range using a Handgun or Rifle before dealing with other enemies. This prevents Mothmants from swarming you and interrupting your attacks. Hildebears are weak to Foie.
