# RAmarl (Human Female Ranger) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **RAmarl** is the ultimate "Jack-of-all-trades" in PSOBB. While she has the lowest base ATP and HP among Rangers, she compensates with the **highest EVP (Evade)** in the game and access to **Level 20 Support Techniques**. This makes her the premier solo Ranger and an invaluable asset to any party.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this plan which assumes the use of **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1145
- **Max ATA:** 241
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 517
- **Max EVP:** 831 (Highest in game)
- **Max MST:** 818

### Material Distribution (250 Cap)
- **Power Materials:** 162
- **Luck Materials:** 63
- **Defense Materials:** 25
- **Mind/Evade:** 0 (EVP and MST will be naturally very high).
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Mandatory to compensate for low base health).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Standard Build:** 5 DEF / 149 POW / 46 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Recommended Evolution:** **Sato** or **Diwari** (Excellent invincibility/buff triggers).
- **Special Evolution:** **Striker Unit** (Required for the *Heaven Striker* special).

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **The Signature:** *Heaven Striker* (Must be paired with *Striker Unit* Mag).
- **Crowd Control:** *Spread Needle* (Essential for locking down mobs with paralysis).
- **Freezing:** *Frozen Shooter* or *Snow Queen* (100% Freeze rate).
- **Single Target DPS:** *Charge Vulcans* (50%+ Hit) or *Dual Bird*.
- **Female Exclusive:** *Rambling May* (Powerful twin-shot weapon).
- **Melee Utility:** *Excalibur* (For high ATP melee situations).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Sweetheart* (Female only; huge stat boosts) or *Stealth Suit* (EVP focus).
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for all stats) or *Ranger Wall* (Excellent mid-game ATA boost).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **V502:** Increases success rate of status specials (Hell, Freeze, Arrest).
3. **Centurion/Ability:** Used to cap all remaining stats.
4. **Adept:** Reduces TP cost and provides useful resistance/stat boosts.

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Greenill (Primary):** The "Ranger ID." Best for hunting *Heaven Striker*, *V101*, and *V502*.
- **Purplenum:** Best for hunting high-end *Mechguns* and *Psycho Wand*.
- **Redria:** Great for general utility and the *Red Ring*.
- **Yellowboze:** Excellent for solo play due to balanced drops and high Hit% chances.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Solo Strategies
- **Support Priority:** Your most important tools are **Zalure (Lv 20)** and **Jellen (Lv 20)**. Zalure is critical to overcome your low base ATP, and Jellen makes you virtually invincible when combined with your high EVP.
- **EVP Tanking:** With optimal gear, RAmarl can "block" or evade the majority of physical attacks in Ultimate difficulty. Focus on maintaining high EVP to survive in the thick of combat.
- **The Freeze & Shred:** A classic RAmarl tactic: Freeze a dangerous enemy (like a Delbiter) with *Frozen Shooter*, then shred it with *Charge Vulcans* or *Heaven Striker*.
- **Technique Mastery:** Use **Resta (Lv 20)** and **Anti (Lv 7)** to keep yourself and your team healthy. Your high MST also makes your offensive techs (up to Lv 20) viable for clearing trash mobs in lower difficulties.
- **Team Role:** You are the **Ultimate Enabler**. Keep the team buffed with Shifta/Deband and keep the enemies debuffed and status-locked.
