# FOnewearl (Newman Female Force) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **FOnewearl** is the "Ultimate Caster" of PSOBB. She possesses the **highest MST (Mental Strength)** and **TP** in the game, making her the most potent single-target nuker. Her unique ability to have **Megid pierce** through enemies and her doubled range for Resta and Anti make her an exceptional support and specialized damage dealer.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
FOnewearl focuses almost exclusively on MST to maximize her technique damage.
- **Max ATP:** 583
- **Max ATA:** 186 (Highest for Forces)
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 390
- **Max EVP:** 883 (Highest for Forces)
- **Max MST:** 1750 (Highest in game)

### Material Distribution (150 Cap - Newman Limit)
- **Mind Materials:** 82
- **Defense Materials:** 42
- **Evade Materials:** 13
- **Luck Materials:** 13
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Mandatory to compensate for lowest HP in game).
- **TP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Standard Build:** 5 DEF / 18 POW / 16 DEX / 161 MIND
- **Pure MST Build:** 5 DEF / 0 POW / 0 DEX / 195 MIND
- **Recommended Evolution:** **Sato** or **Nidra**.
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **The "Nuke":** *Psycho Wand* (Essential for raw power; reduces TP cost via HP).
- **Simple-tech Specialist:** *Summit Moon* (+30% damage to Foie, Zonde, Barta).
- **The Reaper:** *Dark Bridge* (+30% Grants/Megid boost). Essential for piercing Megid.
- **Ranged Utility:** *Slicer of Fanatic* or *Bringer's Rifle* (Demon's special).
- **Tech Utility:** *Magical Piece* (+30% Gi-series damage).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Thirteen* (EVP focus) or *Aura Field*.
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS all stats) or *Simple-tech Merges* (Foie/Barta/Zonde Merge for +30% damage).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Mandatory for stats.
2. **V801:** Essential for casting speed.
3. **Smartlink:** Mandatory for landing Demon's specials with Ranger weapons.
4. **Adept:** Massive stat boost and TP reduction.

## 4. Innate Class Boosts
- **Simple-series Techniques:** +30% Damage (Foie, Zonde, Barta).
- **Megid:** Special property—Megid pierces through multiple enemies.
- **Support Range:** 2x Range for **Resta** and **Anti**.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Solo Strategies
- **Single-Target Nuking:** Use **Foie**, **Zonde**, and **Barta** to pick off high-priority targets. These are your most efficient spells due to your innate +30% boost.
- **Megid Piercing:** Line up enemies in narrow corridors and use **Megid** to clear rows of non-dark enemies with a single cast. Use **Dark Bridge** to increase its success rate.
- **EVP Tanking:** FOnewearl has very high EVP. With optimal gear, you can evade many physical attacks, allowing you to cast safely while under pressure.
- **Resta Range:** Your doubled Resta range means you can heal the entire party from a safe distance.
- **TP Management:** As a Newman, your natural TP regeneration is your best friend. Use your high TP pool to stay in the fight longer than any other class.
- **Team Role:** The "Sage" / Single-Target Specialist. You provide elite support through high-range healing and status removal while delivering devastating single-target technique damage.
