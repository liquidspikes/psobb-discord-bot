# Government Task 5-4
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 5 - VR Temple)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Eliminate high-threat anomalies in VR Temple Beta.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Beta**: Warp to VR Temple Beta.
2. **Locked Arenas**: Clear three consecutive locked rooms.
3. **Hall Slayers**: Defeat the Grass Assassin and Lily mini-boss wave in the grand hall.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Diminians, Grass Assassins, Poison Lilies, Hildebears, Hildeblues.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Always prioritize Poison Lilies first to avoid getting paralyzed or poisoned during massive fights.
