# Government Task 5-5
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 5 - VR Temple)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Defeat Barba Ray to clear the VR Temple simulation.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Boss**: Warp to the VR Temple Boss Portal.
2. **Defeat Barba Ray**: Engage the giant aquatic worm.
3. **Report**: Slay Pig Rays, hit its head, and report back to the Lab.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest permanently sets flag `0x021B`, unlocking VR Spaceship in Free Play!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Barba Ray Boss.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Wipe out the small flying fish (Pig Rays) immediately with Gizonde. Attack its head when it rests on the raft. Run in a circle when it shoots homing lasers.
