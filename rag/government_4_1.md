# Government Task 4-1
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 4 - Ruins)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach the ancient ruins and activate the three monument stones.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp Down**: Warp to Ruins 1.
2. **Delsaber Ambush**: Clear the Delsabers in the first chamber.
3. **Locate Monument 1**: Navigate east and activate the first seal stone.
4. **Locate Monument 2**: Head west, slay the Dark Belras, and activate the second seal stone.
5. **Locate Monument 3**: Move north, activate the third seal stone, and warp back to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dimenians, La Dimenians, So Dimenians, Delsabers, Dark Belras.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Delsabers block front-facing attacks with their shields. Wait for them to swing first, dodge, then counter-attack during recovery, or use magic.
