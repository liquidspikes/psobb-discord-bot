# RAcast (Android Male Ranger) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **RAcast** is the "High-HP Powerhouse" of the Ranger class. He boasts the highest **ATP (Attack Power)** and **HP** among all Rangers, and the highest HP in the entire game. As an Android, he has built-in **Trap Vision**, **natural HP regeneration**, and a **30% bonus** to the success rates of Blizzard, Arrest, and Chaos weapon specials.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this plan which assumes the use of **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1350 (Highest for Rangers)
- **Max ATA:** 224
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 540
- **Max EVP:** 643

### Material Distribution (150 Cap - Android Limit)
- **Power Materials:** 90
- **Defense Materials:** 32
- **Luck Materials:** 28
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Always max this separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Build:** 5 DEF / 148 POW / 47 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**. Since RAcast cannot use Techniques, this is his only method for high-level self-buffing in solo play.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **The Room Clearer:** *Dark Meteor* (The ultimate Ranger weapon; massive AoE damage).
- **The "Nuke":** *Heaven Striker* (Paired with *Striker Unit* Mag).
- **Crowd Control:** *Spread Needle* (Essential for arresting groups).
- **Freezing:** *Frozen Shooter* or *Snow Queen* (100% Freeze rate).
- **Single Target DPS:** *Charge Vulcans* (50%+ Hit) or *Guld Milla*.
- **Melee Utility:** *Excalibur* (For high ATP melee situations).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *D-Parts ver1.01* (Android exclusive; adds +35 ATP) or *DF Field*.
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for all stats) or *Ranger Wall*.

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **V502:** Increases success rate of status specials (Hell, Arrest, Freeze).
3. **Centurion/Ability:** Rounding out any remaining stats.
4. **Adept:** Massive ATA boost for higher difficulty accuracy.

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Purplenum (Primary):** The "Ranger ID." Best for finding *Yasminkov 9000M* and general Ranger gear.
- **Redria:** High utility. Finds *Heaven Striker*, *Spread Needle*, and *Red Ring*.
- **Greenill:** Specifically focused on *Rifles* and high-end Units.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Trap Strategies
- **Trap Mastery:** Use **Freeze Traps** to neutralize dangerous threats like Delbiters or Mini-bosses instantly. Use **Confuse Traps** in large rooms to let enemies weaken each other.
- **Trap-Shooting:** Drop a trap and shoot it with a handgun to trigger it instantly. This is vital for fast-paced room clearing.
- **Special Inflicting:** Take advantage of your 30% bonus to **Arrest** and **Blizzard**. A *Spread Needle* with high success rate is your best friend.
- **Distance Management:** Use your high HP to tank hits when necessary, but use the superior range of *Rifles* and *Dark Meteor* to control the flow of the battle.
- **Team Role:** You are the **Primary Room Clearer and Crowd Controller**. Control the field with traps and status effects, then unleash your massive ATP to finish the fight.
