# Government Task 6-3
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 6 - VR Spaceship)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Deep sweep and security override of VR Spaceship Beta.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Beta Entry**: Teleport to Spaceship Beta.
2. **Slay Clones**: Slay the Pan Arms in the central corridors.
3. **Terminal Access**: Locate and override the security terminal, then report back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gilchichs, Pan Arms, Delsabers, Baranz.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
When Pan Arms split into two separate entities, they become highly vulnerable to elemental magic (Gizonde/Rabarta). Kill them before they recombine.
