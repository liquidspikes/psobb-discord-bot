# Government Task 7-3
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 7 - Central Control Area)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Locate the Mountain Dome Switch in the Mountain Sector.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Mountain Climb**: Enter the Mountain Sector.
2. **Dodge Boulders**: Climb uphill while avoiding rolling boulder traps.
3. **Defeat Mericars**: Slay the Mericars blocking the upper switch path.
4. **Activate**: Hit the Mountain Dome Switch and report back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Mountain Gibbons, Gee, Mericar, Mericus.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Mericars have a close-range spit attack that inflicts instant death (Megid-effect). Never stand directly in front of them; attack from range or behind.
