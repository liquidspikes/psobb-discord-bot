# Government Task 2-3
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 2 - Caves)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Clear the high-infestation path in Caves 3 to secure the passage to the lower core.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Caves 3 Entry**: Warp to Caves 3.
2. **Locked Arena 1**: Slay the Pal Shark swarm in the first locked room.
3. **Slime Room Puzzle**: In the long water corridor, you will encounter Pofuilly Slimes. Clear them out to open the door.
4. **Locked Arena 2**: Slay the 3 Nano Dragons and Grass Assassins in the final double-deck chamber.
5. **Warp Out**: Report back to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Evil Sharks, Pal Sharks, Pofuilly Slimes, Grass Assassins, Nano Dragons.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Pofuilly Slimes can split when hit by physical attacks. You can use a weak weapon to hit a slime multiple times to split it. If a rare red slime (Pouilly Slime) spawns, it has premium rare drops!
