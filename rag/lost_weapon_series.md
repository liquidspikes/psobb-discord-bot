# Lost Weapon Series
**Category**: Multiplayer Farming Quest

## 📋 Mission Objective
Recover lost weapons (Lost Heat Sword, Lost Soul Blade, etc.) within a time limit.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Timer Start**: Warp to designated sector (Forest, Caves, Mines, or Ruins) under a 20-30 min timer.
2. **Room Sweeps**: Clear exceptionally high-density, multi-wave mob spawns in every chamber.
3. **Recover**: Slay the final wave, recover the lost weapon from the secure chest, and return to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Massive high-density spawns of standard sector enemies.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
These quests have the highest mob density on the server. They are the absolute best quests for target-farming normal enemies (such as hunting for rare drops from Tollaws or slimes) because of the sheer quantity of spawns.
