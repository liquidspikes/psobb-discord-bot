# Government Task 6-2
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 6 - VR Spaceship)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Secure the primary docking bay in VR Spaceship Alpha.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Bay Patrol**: Explore the northern sectors of Spaceship Alpha.
2. **Shutdown Barriers**: Locate two console switches that shut down electric barriers.
3. **Defeat Tanks**: Defeat a heavy wave of Garanz and Canadines.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gilchichs, Dubchichs, Canadines, Garanz.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Lure Canadines out of their formations with a handgun or spell, then clear them out before engaging the heavy Garanz.
