# FOmarl (Human Female Force) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **FOmarl** is the "Paladin" of the Force class. She has the highest **HP** and **DFP** of all Forces, making her the most durable. Her unique range boosts for support techniques (Resta, Anti, Shifta, Deband) and her 50% damage boost to Grants define her as the premier Support-Force in PSOBB.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
FOmarl is often built for defense and MST to survive in the thick of combat while supporting the team.
- **Max ATP:** 1002
- **Max ATA:** 170
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 498 (Highest for Forces)
- **Max EVP:** 588
- **Max MST:** 1284

### Material Distribution (250 Cap)
- **Mind Materials:** 165
- **Luck Materials:** 30
- **Evade Materials:** 48
- **Power/Defense:** 7 (Distributed to round out base stats).
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Essential to maximize her role as a tanky Force).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Support Build:** 5 DEF / 68 POW / 48 DEX / 79 MIND
- **Recommended Evolution:** **Sato** or **Nidra** for invincibility triggers.
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **Primary Support:** *Striker of Chao* (Shifta/Deband range boost) or *Glide Divine* (Jellen/Zalure range boost).
- **The Grants Cannon:** *Dark Bridge* or *Vivienne* (to capitalize on her +50% Grants boost).
- **Single Target DPS:** *Rambling May* (Female-only; high damage).
- **Melee Utility:** *Excalibur* (Incredible ATP for a Force).
- **Tech Utility:** *Marina's Bag* (Resta range boost).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Thirteen* (EVP focus) or *Aura Field* (High DFP).
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS all stats) or *Anti-Darkness* (For specific resists).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **V801:** Essential for casting speed.
3. **Adept:** Massive stat boost and resistance, crucial for FOmarl's hybrid role.
4. **V502:** For landing Demon's or Hell specials.

## 4. Innate Class Boosts
- **Grants:** +50% Damage.
- **Support Range:** 2x Range for **Resta**, **Anti**, **Shifta**, and **Deband**.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Solo Strategies
- **The "Paladin" Role:** Use your high defense to stand on the front lines, keeping the party buffed and healed while using **Rambling May** or **Excalibur** to clear trash mobs.
- **Grants Specialist:** On high-defense targets, your **Grants** will hit harder than any other tech. Use it to pick off enemies from a distance.
- **Support Range Dominance:** Because your S/D/Resta/Anti range is doubled, you can support your team from the center of a room without having to chase them down.
- **Zalure Weaving:** Use an S-Rank weapon with **Zalure** to debuff large groups quickly before the Hunters engage.
- **Team Role:** The "White Mage" / Support Pillar. Your presence makes a team nearly invincible through constant, high-range support and status removal.
