# Government Task 5-3
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 5 - VR Temple)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Recover lost diagnostic data chips in VR Temple Alpha.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Alpha**: Return to VR Temple Alpha.
2. **Collect Chips**: Locate 5 glowing data chip items scattered throughout the north and east chambers.
3. **Bear Gate**: Defeat the Hildebear guards in the final room and report back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Diminians, Hildebears, Grass Assassins, Monests.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Freeze Hildebears with Barta/Rabarta or utilize a Slicer to hit them safely from behind cover.
