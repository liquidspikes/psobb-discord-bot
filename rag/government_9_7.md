# Government Task 9-7
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Desert)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Reach the secondary sectors of Subterranean Desert 2.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to Desert 2**: Warp down to Subterranean Desert 2.
2. **Giant Scorpion**: Engage the Girtablulu in the central cavern.
3. **Console Hacks**: Override the three security matrices and report back.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gorans, Pyro Gorans, Girtablulu, Pazuzu.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Girtablulu scorpions fire homing lasers and stinger Megid. Freeze them with Barta/Rabarta or strike their tail core from behind.
