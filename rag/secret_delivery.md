# Secret Delivery
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Caves)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Deliver a top-secret capsule and meet Sue.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Capsule Brief**: Receive capsule, warp to Caves 1.
2. **Meet Sue**: Speak to Sue standing in the second corridor.
3. **CRITICAL CHOICE**: Tell Sue your name if you wish to lock into Sue's story route (required for special endings later).
4. **Delivery**: Deliver capsule to the Caves 1 terminal, defeat the ambushing Grass Assassins, and return.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Sharks, Grass Assassins, Poison Lilies.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
If you want to unlock the full story ending in 'Seek My Master' or 'Soul of a Steel', you must tell Sue your name.
