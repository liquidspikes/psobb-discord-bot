# Magnitude of Metal
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Forest)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Accompany the android Elenor and recover lost military cargo.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Partnership**: Elenor joins your party. Warp to Forest 1.
2. **Traps**: Walk through corridors. Listen to Elenor's audio warnings about hidden traps.
3. **Retrieve Cargo**: Navigate to the northern ruins, defeat the Hildebears, and retrieve the military capsule.
4. **Exit**: Return to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Boomas, Savage Wolves, Hildebears.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Listen to Elenor's audio cues. When she says a trap is near, back up and detonate the mines with a handgun.
