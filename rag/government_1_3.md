# Government Task 1-3
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 1 - Forest)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach the final chamber in Forest 2, defeat the central anomaly (The Dragon), and establish a passage to the Caves.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Path to the Portal**: Brief with the Principal, then warp to Forest 2. Follow the direct, linear path to the north portal room. Clear out the 2 Hildebears to unlock the boss portal.
2. **Enter the Arena**: Step through the red portal to enter the circular underground arena.
3. **Slay the Dragon**: Engage the Dragon boss through its physical, flight, and burrowing phases. Focus your heavy physical attacks on its legs until it collapses.
4. **Report Victory**: After the Dragon dissolves, step into the exit warp, return to Pioneer 2, and speak to the Principal.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest permanently sets flag `0x01F9`, unlocking the Caves warp in Free Play and opening Tier 2 quests!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dragon Boss, Boomas (pre-room), Hildebears (pre-room).

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
**Dragon Strategy**:
* **Underbelly Safe Zone**: Stand directly between or under its feet to avoid its tail swipe and breath attacks. Attack its feet to force it to collapse.
* **Flight Phase**: When it flies, run in a continuous circle around the absolute outer wall of the arena to avoid fireballs.
* **Burrow Phase**: Watch the dirt trails and stay moving to avoid resurfacing slams.
* **Collapse**: When it falls, focus all heavy attacks on its head for double damage.
