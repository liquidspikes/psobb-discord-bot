# Government Task 2-1
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 2 - Caves)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Breach Caves 1, navigate the subterranean labyrinth, and recover the lost diagnostic drone logs.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp Down**: Talk to Nol/Principal, teleport to Caves 1.
2. **Poison Vents**: Move through the narrow magma tunnels. Watch out for wall vents spewing green poison gas. Hit the vents with a weapon to temporarily shut them off.
3. **Relic Room Puzzle**: In the four-way intersection room, step on the three floor switches in numerical order (marked on the floor) to lower the central gate.
4. **Drone Retrieval**: Locate the crashed diagnostic drone in the northwest chamber. Slay the surrounding Grass Assassins and Poison Lilies.
5. **Data Sync**: Interact with the drone terminal, download the logs, and warp back to Pioneer 2 to report.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Evil Sharks, Pal Sharks, Guil Sharks, Grass Assassins, Poison Lilies.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Grass Assassins can charge at high speed and freeze you. Freeze them first with Barta magic or hit them with Foie to deal high fire damage. Slay Poison Lilies from a distance to avoid paralysis.
