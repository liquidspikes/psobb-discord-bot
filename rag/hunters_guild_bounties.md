# Hunters Guild Bounty Board & Mission Guide

As Pioneer 2's Mission Control, you monitor and support the Hunters in completing various Bounties tracked automatically on the server.

## 1. Types of Bounties

### Personal Bounties
- Specially curated tasks tailored to a single Hunter's current character level and class.
- **Common Objectives:** 
  - Finding and holding specific items (e.g., Monomates, Monofluids, technique disks).
  - Consuming specific Materials (e.g., HP Materials, Power Materials).
  - Reaching specific Level milestones (e.g., "Reach Level 17").
- **Redemption:** Tracked automatically via the website and in-game server logic. When conditions are met, players can redeem them on the psobb.io Bounty Board website.

### Team Bounties
- Highly challenging group missions that require cooperation, specific party compositions, or difficulty requirements.
- **Common Directives:**
  - **Diverse Party (HU, RA, FO) Requirement:** Defeating specific bosses (e.g., Olga Flow, De Rol Le, Vol Opt) with a party containing at least one Hunter (HU), one Ranger (RA), and one Force (FO).
  - **Hardcore Carry Requirement:** Escorting lower-level players (typically 10+ levels lower than the carry leader) through major boss fights (e.g., Barba Ray, Gol Dragon).

---

## 2. Team Bounty Rules & Clarifications

### The "Diverse Party" Rule
- **Directive:** "Defeat [Boss Name] with a diverse party (HU, RA, FO)."
- **Rule Explanation:** The party must represent all three combat classes (Hunter, Ranger, Force) simultaneously.
- **Valid Party Sizes & Compositions:**
  - **3-Player Party:** Must be `HU, RA, FO` (exactly one of each).
  - **4-Player Party:** Completely valid as long as all three roles are present! Examples: `HU, HU, RA, FO`, `HU, RA, RA, FO`, `HU, RA, FO, FO`. 
  - **Invalid Parties:** Any party missing one of the three roles (e.g., `HU, HU, RA, RA` or `HU, RA, RA, RA`) does *not* satisfy the diversity requirement.

### The "Hardcore Carry" Rule
- **Directive:** "Hardcore Carry 3 lower-level players (10+ levels lower) through [Boss Name]."
- **Rule Explanation:** The carry leader must run the boss fight with three other players who are each at least 10 levels lower than the leader. This fosters community helping and mentorship.
