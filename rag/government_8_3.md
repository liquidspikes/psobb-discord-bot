# Government Task 8-3
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 8 - Seabed)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Descend into the deepest lab core and defeat Olga Flow.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Olga's Pit**: Step into the central elevator in Seabed Lower to enter the Boss Portal.
2. **Slay Olga Flow**: Defeat Olga Flow Phase 1 (elevator) and Phase 2 (bottom pit).
3. **End of Ep2**: Report to the Lab to finish the Episode 2 campaign!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Olga Flow Boss.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Phase 1: Dodge falling rocks and stay in opposite corners when Olga swings its blade. Phase 2: Hide behind floating drones to block its DNA death rays. Adapt to its shifting body immunities.
