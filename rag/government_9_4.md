# Government Task 9-4
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Crater)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Clear out the hostile nesting grounds in Crater North.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to North**: Warp to Crater North.
2. **Circular Arenas**: Navigate the circular chambers.
3. **Clear Nest**: Defeat the Zu and Boota swarms in the north nest room and report.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x02C0`, unlocking Crater Interior!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Bootas, Zus, Astarks.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Zus spawn in groups here. Use Gizonde to chain shock and stun multiple flying enemies simultaneously.
