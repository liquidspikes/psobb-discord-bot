# Government Task 2-4
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 2 - Caves)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Defeat De Rol Le in the Caven core and unlock Mines.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **To the Raft**: Warp to Caves 3, and enter the green boss portal to board the floating raft.
2. **Defeat De Rol Le**: Engage the giant serpent boss. Slay the purple mines it drops and attack its head shell when it lands on the raft.
3. **Victory**: Report to the Principal.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x0201`, unlocking the Mines warp in Free Play and opening Tier 3 quests!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
De Rol Le Boss.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
**De Rol Le Strategy**:
* **Mines**: Slay the purple mines instantly using Gizonde or wide-swing weapons before they explode.
* **Raft Landing**: When De Rol Le rests its head on the raft, focus physical attacks on its helmet shell to break it, exposing its face for double damage.
* **Laser Attack**: When it charges its purple laser, run to the far left or right of the raft to escape the blast.
