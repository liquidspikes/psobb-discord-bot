# Claiming a Stake
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Forest)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Find the missing land surveyor, Donoph, in the Forest.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Jungle Sector**: Warp to Forest 1 accompanied by Donoph's daughter.
2. **Canyon Search**: Navigate the southern pathways while clearing Savage Wolves.
3. **Rescue**: Locate Donoph resting near the big tree in the central clearing. Defeat the sudden wolf ambush.
4. **Report**: Warp back and report.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Boomas, Goboomas, Savage Wolves.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Wolves will jump quickly. Stay close to Donoph's daughter to protect her from jumping Savage Wolves.
