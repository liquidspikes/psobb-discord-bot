# Government Task 4-2
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 4 - Ruins)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Investigate Ruins 2 and purify the corrupt water filters.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Ruins 2 Entry**: Warp to Ruins 2.
2. **Sorcerer Halls**: Navigate hallways while avoiding traps. Slay Chaos Sorcerers to open locked gates.
3. **Water Filter Room**: Defeat the swarms of Dimenians and Chaos Sorcerers in the central circular room.
4. **Purification**: Activate the filtration console, check data, and report to the Principal.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dimenians, Chaos Sorcerers, Delsabers, Dark Belras.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Chaos Sorcerers cast high-damage spells (Rafoie/Rabarta) using their floating gems. Target them first to disable their magic spam.
