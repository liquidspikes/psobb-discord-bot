# Government Task 9-1
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Crater)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Clear a pathway through Crater East.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Crater Warp**: Warp down to Crater East.
2. **Slay Bootas**: Clear the Initial paths of Bootas.
3. **Unlock West**: Reach the western exit warp and return to report.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x02BD`, unlocking Crater West!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Bootas, Ze Bootas, Ba Bootas, Astarks.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Astarks leap in the air and slam down. When one leaps, run to the side. Ranged attacks and elemental magic are highly effective.
