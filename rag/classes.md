# PSOBB Character Classes - Detailed Reference

## Hunters (HU) - Melee Specialists
Hunters excel in close-quarters combat with high ATP (Attack Power) and HP (Hit Points).

### HUmar (Human Male)
- **Role:** Balanced front-liner with basic technique support.
- **Max Stats:** Highest ATP and HP among Humans.
- **Techniques:** Level 15 max. No Shifta, Deband, Jellen, or Zalure.
- **Pros:** High survivability, good for solo play.
- **Cons:** Lack of support buffs makes them dependent on others or items in high-level play.

### HUnewearl (Newman Female)
- **Role:** Evasive, technique-heavy hybrid Hunter.
- **Max Stats:** Highest EVP (Evasion) in the game.
- **Techniques:** Level 20 max. Full access to S/D/J/Z.
- **Special:** Natural TP regeneration while standing still.
- **Pros:** Excellent solo character due to self-buffing/debuffing.
- **Cons:** Lower HP and ATP compared to other Hunters.

### HUcast (Android Male)
- **Role:** Pure physical powerhouse.
- **Max Stats:** Highest ATP and HP in the game.
- **Techniques:** None.
- **Android Specials:** 
  - Natural HP regeneration while standing still.
  - Immune to Poison and Paralysis.
  - **Traps:** Can use Confuse, Freeze, and Damage traps.
  - **Ultimate Bonus:** +30% boost to status effect activation on weapons.
- **Pros:** Massive damage output.
- **Cons:** Cannot heal or buff with techniques.

### HUcaseal (Android Female)
- **Role:** High-accuracy, agile melee specialist.
- **Max Stats:** Highest ATA (Accuracy) and DFP (Defense) among Hunters.
- **Techniques:** None.
- **Android Specials:** Same as HUcast (HP regen, Traps, Status bonus).
- **Animation:** Faster melee animations compared to HUcast.
- **Pros:** Very reliable hits, high defense.
- **Cons:** Lower raw ATP than HUcast.

## Rangers (RA) - Ranged Specialists
Rangers focus on ATA (Accuracy) and controlling the battlefield from a distance.

### RAmar (Human Male)
- **Role:** Precision sniper with basic technique support.
- **Max Stats:** Highest ATA potential in the game.
- **Techniques:** Level 15 max. No Jellen or Zalure.
- **Pros:** Rarely misses, even on Ultimate difficulty.
- **Cons:** Low HP for a male class; lack of J/Z hurts solo efficiency.

### RAmarl (Human Female)
- **Role:** The ultimate support Ranger.
- **Max Stats:** High MST and EVP.
- **Techniques:** Level 20 max. Full access to S/D/J/Z.
- **Pros:** Best solo Ranger due to buffs/debuffs; great team support.
- **Cons:** Lowest ATP and HP of all Rangers.

### RAcast (Android Male)
- **Role:** Heavy weapons specialist.
- **Max Stats:** Highest ATP and HP among Rangers.
- **Techniques:** None.
- **Android Specials:** HP regen, Traps, Status bonus.
- **Pros:** Deals massive damage with Launchers and Shots.
- **Cons:** Lower ATA than human Rangers.

### RAcaseal (Android Female)
- **Role:** Defensive tank Ranger.
- **Max Stats:** Highest DFP in the game. High ATA.
- **Techniques:** None.
- **Android Specials:** HP regen, Traps, Status bonus.
- **Pros:** Extremely hard to kill; very accurate.
- **Cons:** Lower ATP compared to RAcast.

## Forces (FO) - Technique Specialists
Forces rely on MST (Mental Strength) to deal technique damage and support the team.

### FOmar (Human Male)
- **Role:** The "Battle Mage" (Hybrid melee/caster).
- **Max Stats:** Highest ATP among Forces.
- **Techniques:** Level 30 max.
- **Passive Bonuses:**
  - 30% damage boost to Gi-series techniques (Gifoie, Gizonde, Gibarta).
  - Shifta & Deband range +100%.
- **Pros:** Can deal respectable melee damage while casting.
- **Cons:** Lowest HP and DFP in the game; fragile.

### FOmarl (Human Female)
- **Role:** The "Paladin" (Support/Weapon specialist).
- **Max Stats:** Highest HP and DFP among Forces.
- **Techniques:** Level 30 max.
- **Passive Bonuses:**
  - 2x range for Resta, Anti, Shifta, and Deband.
  - Grantz damage +50%.
- **Pros:** Excellent survival and team support; great with Slicers/Bows.
- **Cons:** Lower MST than Newmans.

### FOnewm (Newman Male)
- **Role:** The Area-of-Effect (AoE) nuker.
- **Max Stats:** High MST and TP.
- **Techniques:** Level 30 max.
- **Passive Bonuses:**
  - 30% damage boost to Gi-series and Ra-series techniques.
- **Special:** Natural TP regeneration while standing still.
- **Pros:** Clears rooms of enemies faster than any other class.
- **Cons:** Lower single-target damage compared to FOnewearl.

### FOnewearl (Newman Female)
- **Role:** The ultimate single-target caster and healer.
- **Max Stats:** Highest MST and TP in the game.
- **Techniques:** Level 30 max.
- **Passive Bonuses:**
  - 30% damage boost to Simple-series techniques (Foie, Zonde, Barta).
  - 2x range for Resta and Anti.
  - **Megid:** Pierces through enemies.
- **Special:** Natural TP regeneration while standing still.
- **Pros:** Incredible single-target damage and healing efficiency.
- **Cons:** Very low HP and DFP.
