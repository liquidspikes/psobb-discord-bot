# Government Task 6-4
**Category**: Episode 2 Government Quest (Tier 6 - VR Spaceship)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Survive the Spaceship Beta combat trial.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Beta Arenas**: Teleport to Spaceship Beta.
2. **Consecutive Fights**: Clear 6 locked combat rooms in a row.
3. **Report**: Defeat the final dual Garanz and Delsaber spawn and report to Nol.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gilchichs, Dubchichs, Delsabers, Garanz, Baranz.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Stay mobile, make good use of traps, and prioritize freezing the Delsabers so you can focus on the Garanz tanks.
