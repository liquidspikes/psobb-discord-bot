# Government Task 2-2
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 2 - Caves)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Explore Caves 2 and activate three local relay consoles to map the deeper layout.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Caves 2 Entry**: Teleport to Caves 2.
2. **Find Relay A**: Navigate east. You will face Nano Dragons. Activate the first terminal in the circular water chamber.
3. **Find Relay B**: Move west through the dark rooms. Slay the Poison Lilies guarding the central switch, then activate Relay B.
4. **Find Relay C**: Head south. Slay the Hildeblues blocking the gateway, enter the southern chamber, and activate Relay C.
5. **Report to Pioneer**: Warp back to Pioneer 2 and speak to the Principal.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Evil Sharks, Pal Sharks, Poison Lilies, Nano Dragons, Hildeblues.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
**Megid Alert**: In Hard difficulty and above, Poison Lilies spit **Megid** which causes instant death. Carry **Scape Dolls** in your inventory and prioritize killing Lilies with a long-range Rifle or Handgun.
