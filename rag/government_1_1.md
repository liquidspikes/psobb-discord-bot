# Government Task 1-1
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 1 - Forest)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Investigate Forest 1 anomalies, clear the primary landing sector, and safely recover and protect an injured Pioneer 2 scout.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Pioneer 2 Briefing**: Talk to the Principal in the central chamber, receive your orders, and step onto the central warp to teleport down to Forest 1.
2. **Sector Alpha Entry**: Move south through the initial clearing. You will be ambushed by 3 Boomas. Clear them and move through the unlocked doorway.
3. **North Corridor Puzzle**: Navigate the winding north corridor. Step on the floor switch in the small eastern alcove to deactivate the electric laser fence blocking the western passage.
4. **Rescue Site**: In the large circular courtyard, you will find the injured Scout NPC, Rupert. Slay the immediate wave of Boomas.
5. **Survive the Siege**: Stand ground and protect Rupert from a multi-wave ambush: Wave 1 consists of 4 Savage Wolves; Wave 2 consists of 2 Goboomas and 1 Hildebear.
6. **Extraction**: Once the area is secured, speak to Rupert, take the final teleporter back to Pioneer 2, and report to the Principal.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Boomas, Goboomas, Savage Wolves, Hildebears, Monests, Rag Rappies.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Savage Wolves will circle you before charging. Stand with your back against a wall or obstacle, and use Gizonde or a Slicer weapon to hit multiple wolves simultaneously when they approach. Use fire magic (Foie) on Boomas.
