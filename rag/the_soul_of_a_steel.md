# The Soul of a Steel
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Mines)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Escort Mome to Mines 1 and meet Dr. Montague.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Mines Escort**: Warp to Mines 1 with Mome.
2. **Find Montague**: Slay Dubchics and locate Dr. Montague.
3. **Sue Route Check**: If on Sue's route, Montague will recognize your name and reveal details about Sue's true mission.
4. **Ambush**: Slay the Garanz and Dubchics, then exit.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gillchics, Dubchics, Garanz, Canadines.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Focus on the Dubchic Switches quickly to disable Dubchics so they don't corner Mome.
