# Government Task 4-4
**Category**: Episode 1 Government Quest (Tier 4 - Ruins)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Defeat the primordial darkness, Dark Falz.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to the Altar**: Warp to Ruins 3 and step into the central circular warp.
2. **Slay Dark Falz**: Defeat the Darvant swarm, the giant serpent form, and the final humanoid form.
3. **Victory**: Report to the Principal to finish the main Episode 1 story!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Dark Falz Boss.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
**Dark Falz Strategy**:
* **Phase 1**: Clear Darvants with wide-swing spells (Rabarta/Gizonde).
* **Phase 2**: Run around the edge to dodge ice spikes and light beams.
* **Phase 3**: Do NOT attack when a blue line connects Falz to you (damage reflection). Equip high light-resistance.
