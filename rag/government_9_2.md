# Government Task 9-2
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Crater)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Explore Crater West and locate the seismic sensor terminal.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Warp to West**: Warp to Crater West.
2. **Zu Ambush**: Defeat the flying Zus in the canyons.
3. **Sync Data**: Go to the central sinkhole, activate the seismic terminal, and return.
**IMPORTANT**: Clearing this quest sets flag `0x02BE`, unlocking Crater South!

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Bootas, Zus, Astarks, Sand Rappies.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Zus dive bomb and spit wind blades. Use ranged weapons or Zonde magic to knock them down when they fly low.
