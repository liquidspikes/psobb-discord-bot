# Government Task 9-6
**Category**: Episode 4 Government Quest (Desert)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Navigate the dark caverns of Subterranean Desert 1.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Desert Entry**: Warp to Subterranean Desert 1.
2. **Lanterns**: Hit wall lanterns to light up dark chambers, and avoid quicksand traps.
3. **Goran Ambush**: Slay the teleporting Gorans in the central hall.
4. **Exit**: Find the elevator shaft and report back to Pioneer 2.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gorans, Goran Detonators, Sand Rappies, Lizards.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
Gorans teleport behind you. When you hear a teleport sound, step forward immediately to dodge, then cast Rabarta to freeze them.
