# HUnewearl (Newman Female Hunter) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **HUnewearl** is the most versatile Hunter in PSOBB. As a Newman-Hunter hybrid, she combines respectable melee power with the highest technique levels of any non-Force class (Level 20). She is often considered the most self-sufficient solo character due to her ability to buff, debuff, and heal at high levels while tanking with her high EVP.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this plan which assumes the use of **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1238
- **Max ATA:** 199
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 583
- **Max EVP:** 812
- **Max MST:** 1177

### Material Distribution (150 Cap - Newman Limit)
- **Power Materials:** 99
- **Luck Materials:** 28
- **Defense Materials:** 16
- **Mind Materials:** 7
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap).
- **TP Materials:** 125 (Separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Build:** 5 DEF / 95 POW / 100 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Recommended Evolution:** **Sato** or **Nidra** (Essential for Invincibility triggers).
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)** for extra S/D power in solo play.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **Primary Melee:** *Excalibur* (Incredible 3-target damage).
- **High Crit/Speed:** *Daylight Scar* (Excellent for single targets).
- **Ranged DPS:** *M&A60 Vise* (Mechguns; requires high Hit%).
- **Special Inflicting:** *Slicer of Fanatic* (Demon's special) or *ES Slicer* with Arrest/Hell.
- **Boss Killer:** *Dark Flow* (Special waves at low HP).
- **Tech Utility:** *Twin Blaze* (Special casts Gifoie to stagger enemies).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *Blue Odoshi Violet Nimaidou (BOVN)* or *Thirteen*.
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for stats and ATA) or *Kasami Bracer* (ATP boost).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **Smartlink:** Mandatory for melee characters using ranged weapons in Ultimate (negates accuracy penalty).
3. **Adept:** Used to round out missing stats and provide massive TP reduction.
4. **V502:** Increases the success rate of status specials (Hell, Demon's, Arrest).

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Skyly (Primary):** The definitive "Hunter ID." Best for finding *Excalibur* and *Sealed J-Sword*.
- **Purplenum:** Best for hunting high-end *Mechguns* (M&A60 Vise).
- **Redria:** Great for general utility, *God/Battle*, and *Red Ring*.
- **Whitill:** Excellent for *Slicers* and *V502*.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Solo Strategies
- **The Buff Cycle:** Always maintain Level 20 **Shifta/Deband**. Use **Jellen/Zalure (Lv 20)** on every mob. Zalure effectively acts as a massive ATP boost for you.
- **Newman Special:** You regenerate TP while standing still. Use this to maintain infinite **Resta (Lv 20)** between combat encounters.
- **Tech Staggering:** Use low-level **Rabarta** to freeze enemies or **Gifoie** to stagger them before closing the distance for melee.
- **Special Usage:** Your decent ATA and access to V502 make you an effective user of **Demon's** or **Hell** specials. Use these on high-HP targets like Delbiters or Mini-bosses.
- **Team Role:** You are the **Tanky Support-Hybrid**. You can fill in for a Force by providing S/D/J/Z while holding the front line. Your high EVP makes you surprisingly durable in the thick of a fight.
