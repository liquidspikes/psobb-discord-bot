# RAcaseal (Android Female Ranger) - Deep Dive Reference

## 1. Class Overview
The **RAcaseal** is the "High-DFP Tank" of the Ranger class. She possesses the **highest natural DFP (Defense)** in the entire game and very high ATA. As an Android, she has built-in **Trap Vision**, **natural HP regeneration**, and a **30% bonus** to the success rates of Blizzard, Arrest, and Chaos weapon specials. Her animations are often considered faster and smoother than those of the RAcast.

## 2. Max Stats Blueprint (Level 200)
To achieve "Yellow Stats" (Max) at Level 200, follow this plan which assumes the use of **V101** and **Red Ring**:
- **Max ATP:** 1301
- **Max ATA:** 231
- **Max LCK:** 100
- **Max DFP:** 601 (Highest in game)
- **Max EVP:** 735

### Material Distribution (150 Cap - Android Limit)
- **Power Materials:** 51
- **Defense Materials:** 54
- **Luck Materials:** 45
- **HP Materials:** 125 (Always max this separate cap).

### Optimal Mag Stats
- **Build:** 5 DEF / 150 POW / 45 DEX / 0 MIND
- **Mandatory Photon Blast:** **Mylla & Youlla (M&Y)**. This is her primary source of Shifta/Deband buffs for solo play.

## 3. Endgame Arsenal (Best-in-Slot)
### Weapons
- **The Room Clearer:** *Dark Meteor* (The ultimate Ranger weapon; massive AoE damage).
- **The "Nuke":** *Heaven Striker* (Paired with *Striker Unit* Mag).
- **Crowd Control:** *Spread Needle* (Essential for locking down mobs).
- **Freezing:** *Frozen Shooter* or *Snow Queen* (100% Freeze rate).
- **Single Target DPS:** *Charge Vulcans* (50%+ Hit) or *Guld Milla*.
- **Precision:** *Raygun/Laser* with Hell or Arrest (takes advantage of high ATA).

### Armor & Shields
- **Armor:** *D-Parts ver1.01* (Android exclusive; adds +35 ATP) or *DF Field*.
- **Shield:** *Red Ring* (BiS for all stats).

### Units (The Four Slots)
1. **V101:** (Speed, ATP, ATA, LCK boost). Absolute mandatory.
2. **V502:** Increases success rate of status specials (Hell, Arrest, Freeze).
3. **Centurion/Ability:** Rounding out any remaining stats.
4. **Adept:** Used for resistance boosts and ATA topping.

## 4. Section ID Recommendations
- **Purplenum (Primary):** The "Ranger ID." Best for finding *Yasminkov 9000M* and general Ranger gear.
- **Redria:** High utility. Finds *Heaven Striker*, *Spread Needle*, and *Red Ring*.
- **Greenill:** Focused on *Rifles* and high-end units.

## 5. Advanced Combat & Trap Strategies
- **Ultimate Tanking:** Use your **highest-in-game DFP** to hold positions that would kill other Rangers. You can often stand your ground while clearing a room with *Dark Meteor*.
- **Trap Mastery:** Use **Freeze Traps** to freeze-lock dangerous mobs and **Confuse Traps** to create chaos in high-density areas.
- **Precision Status Inflicting:** Your high ATA makes you excellent at using **Hell** and **Arrest** specials. You will land status effects more consistently than the RAcast.
- **Trap-Shooting:** Drop and shoot traps instantly to clear pathing and neutralize hidden threats.
- **Team Role:** You are the **Sturdy Controller and Precision Damage Dealer**. You provide a safe environment for your team through superior defense and reliable status effects.
