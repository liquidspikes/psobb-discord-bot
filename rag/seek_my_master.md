# Seek My Master
**Category**: Hunter's Guild Side Quest (Mines)

## 📋 Mission Objective
Rescue three missing students of a legendary master.

## 🚶 Step-by-Step Walkthrough
1. **Mines Entry**: Warp to Mines 1 and 2.
2. **Find Ash**: Locate Ash in Mines 1 and clear the Sinow Beat wave.
3. **Find Sue**: Find Sue in Mines 2 and defeat the Garanz and Dubchic swarm.
4. **Find Rupika**: Locate Rupika in Mines 2 and clear the final heavy robot ambush.

## 👾 Notable Enemy Spawns
Gillchics, Dubchics, Canadines, Garanz, Sinow Beats.

## 💡 Advanced Tactical Pro-Tips
This quest is highly difficult. Bring ample recovery items, as you will face multiple Sinow Beats and Garanz simultaneously.
